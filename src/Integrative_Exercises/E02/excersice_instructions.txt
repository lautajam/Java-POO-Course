Exercise: VideoGames

Create a class called VideoGame, that has the following attributes:
    Code, title, console,quantityPlayers, category.
Take into account all its attributes, constructors, getters and setters methods.

* Create an ArrayList of type VideoGame. Create 5 video-games and save them in the collection.

* Traverse the created ArrayList and display the title, console and number of players on the screen:
    The title, console and number of players of the stored video games.

* Change the name and number of players of two video games.
    Display the data of all the video games after the change.

* Scroll through the ArrayList and display on screen only the video games that are from the "Nintendo 64" console.
