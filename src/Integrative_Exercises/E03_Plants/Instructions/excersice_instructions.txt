Exercise: Plants

Create a Plant class with the attributes: name, stem height, hasLeaves, ideal climate.
With its corresponding methods and constructors.

* Create its child classes that share its attributes and methods:

	o Tree: variety, trunk type, trunk radius,
		color, type of leaves.
	o Flower: average number of petals, color of pistil,
		color of petals, flower variety, blooming season.
	o Shrub: widthShrub, isDomestic, varietyShrub, leavesColor, pruning

* Once the classes are created, create the following methods:
    o Tree: method to display a message on the screen saying "Hello I am a tree".
    o Flower: method to display a message on the screen that says "Hello I am a flower".
    o Shrub: method to display a message on the screen saying "Hello I am a bush".

* Create in the Main an object of each child class. Call the 3 methods from each object.
